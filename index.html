About CheckMate

CheckMate is a powerful browser extension designed to help you navigate the complex world of online news. With the rise of misinformation, it's more important than ever to have a reliable tool that can assist you in identifying fake news and understanding the political leanings of various news sources.

Features

Fake News Detection

Our advanced algorithm analyzes articles to identify potential misinformation.

Political Spectrum Analysis

Understand the political leanings of news sources to get a balanced perspective.

Easy to Use

Seamlessly integrates with your browser for a hassle-free experience.
